Vim

download vim

Vim (a contraction of Vi IMproved) is a clone, with additions, of Bill Joy's vi text editor program for Unix.

History

Vim's forerunner, Stevie (ST Editor for VI Enthusiasts), was created by Tim Thompson for the Atari ST in 1987 and further developed by Tony Andrews and G.R. Walter.

Basing his work on Stevie, Bram Moolenaar began working on Vim for the Amiga computer in 1988, with the first public release (Vim v1.14) in 1991.

at the time of its first release, the name "Vim" was an acronym for "Vi IMitation", but this changed to "Vi IMproved"
